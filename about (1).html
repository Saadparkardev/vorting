College Campus Voting App

Welcome to the College Campus Voting App! This platform allows students to participate in the democratic process by voting for their representatives and important campus issues.

Rules and Regulations

  • All students must be registered to vote in order to participate.
  • Voting is open to all students enrolled in the current semester.
  • Each student is allowed one vote per election.
  • Voting will take place online through the app during the designated voting period.
  • Students must verify their identity using their student ID and password.
  • Any form of tampering or fraud will result in disqualification from voting.
  • Results will be announced within 24 hours after the voting period ends.
